About Us

Par5 Escapes: Exceptional Golf Holidays Made Easy

Par5 Escapes is a premier golf travel agency based in the UK, offering exceptional golf holidays across Europe, Africa, and the Middle East. As a full-service travel agency, Par5 Escapes ensures that every aspect of your trip is meticulously planned and managed, providing you with a seamless and memorable golf experience. We work closely with fully bonded tour operators to ensure that all aspects of your golf holiday are fully covered and protected. From flights and transfers to accommodation and tee times, we are your one point of contact for every element of your trip. Our dedicated team is always available to answer any questions, big or small, that you may have along the way. As golf specialists, we leverage our firsthand experience and in-depth knowledge of golf destinations to build the perfect itinerary tailored to your preferences. We pride ourselves on our customer service, understanding that it’s the small details that make a big difference in creating a truly memorable holiday.

Our Team

At Par5 Escapes, our team is dedicated to creating the best golfing experiences for our customers. Leading our team is Simon Wakefield, our Golf Director, who played on the European Tour for 18 years and has played over 1,000 golf courses around the world. Simon’s deep knowledge of golf courses worldwide is invaluable in crafting exceptional golf holidays tailored to each customer’s preferences. Our sales team is comprised entirely of avid golfers and single handicaps, each with extensive experience in the golf travel industry. Their passion for golf and firsthand knowledge of the sport ensures that every detail of your trip is meticulously planned and executed, providing you with a seamless and unforgettable golf holiday.
